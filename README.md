
# Dr. David Andai — Portfolio

Personal portfolio for David Andai, a medical doctor working at the intersection of brain health, neuroscience and machine learning. The site covers his research focus areas: brain health burden, brain health determinants, and brain capital, alongside his clinical experience, peer-reviewed publications, Neural Dome AI projects, skills and certifications and affiliations. His MNeuroSc at UCT and MINDS 2026 scholarship.

Built with React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS.
